First Aid Quick Guide
🫀 CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

1. Push hard and fast in the center of the chest (100-120 pushes a minute).
2. Give 2 rescue breaths after every 30 pushes if trained.
3. Continue until medical help arrives or the person starts breathing.

🩸 Severe Bleeding

1. Apply direct pressure to the wound using a clean cloth.
2. Maintain continuous pressure for at least 5 minutes.
3. Do not remove the cloth; add more on top if blood soaks through.

🗣️ Choking

1. Stand behind the person and wrap your arms around their waist.
2. Make a fist and place it just above their navel.
3. Perform quick, upward thrusts (Heimlich maneuver) until the object is expelled.
